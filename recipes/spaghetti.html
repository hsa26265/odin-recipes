Hanna’s Famous GF Fettucini

Your New Weeknight Staple!

Fettucini Bolognese has got to be one of my favorite, go-to weeknight dinners. Robust in flavor and deeply filling, this dish is the perfect choice when planning your meals. My bolognese focuses on meaty, umami flavours that pack a serious punch. Mushrooms and artichoke hearts help enhance the flavours of the ground beef alongside the onion and garlic.

Below, you'll find the ingredients and steps that make this recipe come to life!

Ingredients You’ll Need:

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb italian sausage
  • 1/2 white onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of mushrooms, minced
  • 1/4 cup marinated artichoke hearts, minced
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrot, should be very short, small and fine!
  • 1/4 red cooking wine (any red you don't mind using in this recipe rather than drinking)
  • 2 heap tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed red tomatoes (usually go for those without added salt or herbs)
  • 2-3 tbsp italian seasoning (can also do a mix of dried thyme, oregano and parsley here)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Barilla GF fettucini pasta
  • Freshly shredded parmesan cheese & Basil (to top)

How to Make Our Pasta:

  1. Start by finely dicing half a white onion and mincing your garlic, mushrooms, and artichoke hearts.
  2. Heat a pan over medium-heat add in your ground beef and sausage. Chop up the meat in the pan and allow to brown. Once browned, remove to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Add a little olive oil to the pan if needed. Toss in your onions, and sautee ‘til soft (about 5 minutes).
  4. Once your onions have softened, add in your mushrooms, artichokes, and garlic, sauteeing for about 1 min.
  5. Add in your tomato paste and allow to slightly caramelize (about 1 minute). Then add in your wine to deglaze the pan. Let reduce to half.
  6. When your wine has reduced, add in your can of crushed tomatoes and seasonings.
  7. Allow sauce to simmer for about 15 minutes (cook your pasta according to box instructions during this time.)
  8. When your pasta has finished, add 1/4 cup of the pasta water to your sauce.
  9. Voila! Serve and enjoy!
