Gluten-free Kanelbullar

My favorite (GF) Swedish Kanelbullar!

Kanelbullar is a symbol of my childhood. Whether for breakfast, fika, or just for something sweet, kanelbullar is the perfect baked treat for every occasion.

This is paragraph two of some descriptive text for the recipe.

For the Buns:

  • 100 g butter
  • 5 dl milk
  • 4-6 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 dl sugar
  • Â½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp cardamom
  • All-purpose GF Flour (King Arthur has a GREAT one)

The Filling:

  • 150 g butter (let butter come to room temperature)
  • 1 dl sugar
  • 2 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 egg for egg wash
  • pearl sugar

How to Make the Buns!

  1. Melt the butter and whisk in the milk. Add in active dry yeast.
  2. Add in sugar, cardamom, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, add flour, and then slowly whisk in the milk solution. You may want to add more flour until the consistency feels right.
  4. Leave dough to rise for about 30 minutes to an hour.
  5. Roll out your dough into a rectangular shape (FYI this stuff is STICKY, strongly recommend using a non stick mat).
  6. Add your filling: Spread room temp butter across your rolled out dough.
  7. Sprinkle on your sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Cut the dough into strips, long ways, and roll (be very careful, the dough is fragile).
  9. Bake at 190 degrees celcius for 15-20 minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
